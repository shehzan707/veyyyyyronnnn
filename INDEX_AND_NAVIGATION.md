## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Where is the home page template?
**A**: `resources/views/shop/home.blade.php` (646 lines)

**Reference**: [FILE_MANIFEST.md](FILE_MANIFEST.md) - Section: "Main Template File"

---

### Q: Where are the poster images?
**A**: `public/posters/[category]/[brand-name].html` (or `.jpg`/`.png` after replacement)

**Reference**: [FILE_MANIFEST.md](FILE_MANIFEST.md) - Section: "Poster Image Directories"

---

### Q: How do I change the gold color?
**A**: Find `#d4af37` and replace with your color throughout the CSS

**Reference**: [LUXURY_HOME_PAGE_DOCUMENTATION.md](LUXURY_HOME_PAGE_DOCUMENTATION.md) - Section: "Customization Guide"

---

### Q: How many brands are featured?
**A**: 25+ luxury brands across 7 categories

**Reference**: [HOME_PAGE_VISUAL_SUMMARY.md](HOME_PAGE_VISUAL_SUMMARY.md) - Section: "Brand Categories"

---

### Q: Can I use the placeholder images or must I add real ones?
**A**: Both! Placeholders work fine for testing. Replace with real images when ready for production.

**Reference**: [POSTER_IMAGES_IMPLEMENTATION.md](POSTER_IMAGES_IMPLEMENTATION.md) - Section: "Option A vs Option B"

---

### Q: How do I optimize images before uploading?
**A**: Use TinyJPG, Squoosh, or ImageMagick. Target: 150-300KB per image

**Reference**: [POSTER_IMAGES_IMPLEMENTATION.md](POSTER_IMAGES_IMPLEMENTATION.md) - Section: "Step 2: Optimize Images for Web"

---

### Q: What's the recommended image size?
**A**: 400-600px width, 500-800px height (aspect ratio ~0.74:1)

**Reference**: [POSTER_IMAGES_IMPLEMENTATION.md](POSTER_IMAGES_IMPLEMENTATION.md) - Section: "Image Specifications"

---

### Q: How many grid columns on mobile?
**A**: 2-3 columns on mobile, 4-5 on tablet, 6 on desktop

**Reference**: [HOME_PAGE_VISUAL_SUMMARY.md](HOME_PAGE_VISUAL_SUMMARY.md) - Section: "Responsive Design Example"

---

### Q: What CSS frameworks are used?
**A**: None! Pure CSS with Grid and Flexbox. No external dependencies.

**Reference**: [LUXURY_HOME_PAGE_DOCUMENTATION.md](LUXURY_HOME_PAGE_DOCUMENTATION.md) - Section: "CSS Features & Styling"

---

### Q: How do I test hover effects on mobile?
**A**: Use browser DevTools device emulation or test on actual devices

**Reference**: [HOME_PAGE_QUICK_GUIDE.md](HOME_PAGE_QUICK_GUIDE.md) - Section: "Testing Checklist"

---

### Q: What browsers are supported?
**A**: Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+

**Reference**: [FILE_MANIFEST.md](FILE_MANIFEST.md) - Section: "Browser Support"
