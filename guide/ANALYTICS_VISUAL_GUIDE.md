## 📊 DATA DICTIONARY

### KPI Definitions
| Metric | Definition | Formula |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| Total Revenue | Sum of all order amounts | Σ(orders.total_amount) |
| Total Orders | Count of all orders | COUNT(orders) |
| AOV | Average per transaction | Total Revenue / Orders |
| Growth % | Month-over-month change | (Current - Previous) / Previous × 100 |

### Chart Data
| Chart | Data Source | Aggregation |
|-------|-------------|-------------|
| Daily Sales | Orders by hour | GROUP BY hour |
| Top Products | OrderItems by product | GROUP BY product_id |
| Categories | OrderItems by category | GROUP BY category_id |
| Monthly | Orders by date | GROUP BY DATE(created_at) |
| Yearly | Orders by month | GROUP BY MONTH(created_at) |
| Revenue/Orders | Orders by date | GROUP BY DATE(created_at) |
| Customers | Users by created date | GROUP BY DATE(created_at) |
| Low Stock | Products where stock ≤ 10 | WHERE stock <= 10 |

## 🚨 ALERT SCENARIOS

### 🔴 CRITICAL: Revenue Down >25%
```
Actions:
1. Check external factors (holiday, competitor)
2. Review product quality issues
3. Analyze customer feedback
4. Consider promotional campaign
5. Review pricing strategy
```

### 🟡 WARNING: AOV Declining
```
Actions:
1. Review product mix
2. Analyze customer segments
3. Check competition pricing
4. Implement bundle promotions
5. Review order thresholds
```

### 🟡 WARNING: Low Stock > 3 Items
```
Actions:
1. Urgent reorder
2. Limit quantities to prevent stockout
3. Adjust forecasting model
4. Contact supplier
5. Consider price increase
```
